The Bay Pirate

2005 34' Yellowfin

Powered by triple 300hp Yamaha's and a 3-owner boat we knew we were going to have some cosmetic work to be done, but only 100hrs on the new motors along with new fuel tanks.

In 2015, we contacted Live Wire Electronics in Myrtle Beach, SC about getting the boat re-wired. We decided to get a new sound system via J.L. Audio, new gps and sonar from Garmin Marine. Along with new VHF radio's from iCom America and a Fusion Entertainment receiver.

In the spring of 2017, we took the Yellowfin to Wrap's Ink in Murrells Inlet, SC. We had them design the new wrap, and remove the previous owner's PAST DUE logos.

William "Frollie" Hughes

Captain

Team Captain and Senior Angler. Born and raised in Ocean Isle Beach NC, he's always been on or near the water. Starting back in the 60's with surfing the break at the pier the saltwater has been in his blood for a long time. He started honing his king mackerel skills on the piers at Johnny Mercer and Crystal Pier on Wrightsville Beach. Back in the early 70's while in college and transfered that back to Ocean Isle pier when moved he moved back to Brunswick County.

Around 2000, he started fishing with "Playin Hooky" fishing team in king mackerel touranments in the Southern Kingfish Association and the old US Anglers.

Josh Hughes

Co-Captain, Social media and Website designer

Josh Hughes, born and raised in Ocean Isle also, son of "Frollie" came into fishing in the late 2000's mainly fun fishing on their 19ft Pioneer. After catching a couple decent kings, he asked about fishing tournaments. The first tournament that they entered was the U.S. Open and caught a 27.8lb fish that ended up tieing them for 55th place.

From there, his love for the saltwater has been multipled with wanting to be on the water in touranments looking for big kings. He is our social media correspondent, web page developer and video editor, and jack of all trades on the boat.

Patti Hughes

Lady Angler, photographer, videographer

Patti is our lady angler, on board photographer, videographer. Most times she is found behind the camera getting those high quality photos. When the bite is going on, she will help clear lines and occasionally fight a fish while everyone is tied up. If not on the boat, she is watching her grandchildren while Josh fishes, and brings them to weigh in to see what was caught that day. She's our all-around helper and a professional accountant owning her own business since 1983.

Josh Benton

Crew

Born and raised in Whiteville, NC, Josh Benton has a draw to the saltwater so much, that any weekend he's not working at OVM Finicial and taking care of his little boy. He can be found in his bay boat chasing drum, trout or flounder. During college, he played baseball with Josh Hughes they had stayed in touch over the years and talked about fishing. After doing some near-shore fishing with his dad and family members, he wanted to do more offshore fishing. After much discussion with Josh Hughes, he joined the team in 2013. Since joing the team, his enthusiasm and humor, keeps the team laughing wether on days where the bite is a fast or slow bite. There's always a "Rember this" moment. If you get a chance, ask him about his "Cobia stories" from fishing with our team.

Jon Sweeney

Crew

Jon Sweeney aka "Swabby" joined the team in 2016. Jon workes with Josh Benton at OVM Finicial, where you can find them talking fishing whether inshore or offshore. Jon brings a wealth of knowledge from his days of working on head boats bottom fishing. On the boat you can find him with the cast net early in the mornings looking for bait or eager to be trying to jig up bait in the area we are fishing, and keeping the bait spread free of tangles. When not on the boat, he can be found chasing drum, trout, flounder or on the softball field.
